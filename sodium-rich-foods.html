Understanding Sodium in Your Diet

Sodium is an essential electrolyte crucial for optimal bodily functionâ€”maintaining fluid balance, supporting nerve transmission, and enabling proper muscle contraction. Your individual sodium needs are as unique as you are, varying based on factors like activity level, sweat rate, genetics, and health goals.

While conventional wisdom often focuses on limiting sodium, many active individuals actually benefit from carefully calibrated sodium intake. This reference guide lists common sodium-rich foods organized by category. Understanding your personal sodium sources helps you make strategic dietary choices, allowing you to fine-tune your electrolyte balance for peak performance, recovery, and well-being.
